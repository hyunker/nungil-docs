# Privacy Policy

Nungil (hereinafter referred to as "the App") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles your information.

## 1. Information We Collect and How We Collect It

The App does not require user registration, nor does it collect or transmit personally identifiable information to external servers. However, to provide the core functionalities, the App requests access to the following data locally on your device
Additionally, since it does not create a user account, there is no need for a separate account deletion process, and all local data, including emergency contacts, stored on the device are permanently deleted as soon as the app is deleted.

- **Camera Permission**: The App uses the front camera in real-time to track eye movements and blinks for interaction. This data is **processed locally on your device and is never stored, recorded, or transmitted to any external servers.**
- **Contacts Permission**: We request access to your contacts solely to allow you to select emergency contacts. All contact data is stored locally on your device.
- **Phone Call Permission**: Required to facilitate emergency calls to your designated contacts directly from the App.
- **SMS Permission**: Required to send emergency text messages to your designated contacts.

## 2. Purpose of Information Processing and Retention

The App does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. The permissions requested are strictly used in real-time for the core functionality of the App (augmentative and alternative communication via eye-tracking and emergency alerts). Deleting the App will remove all associated local settings from your device.

## 3. Third-Party Data Sharing

Since we do not collect or store any personal information, we do not share, sell, or disclose your data to any third parties.

## 4. Device Permissions

The App requires the following permissions to function properly:

- **[Required] Camera**: Essential for eye-tracking and blink detection.
- **[Optional] Contacts, Phone, SMS**: Used for emergency contact features. (While optional, denying these will restrict the emergency features.)
